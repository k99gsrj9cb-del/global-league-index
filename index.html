How ratings are calculated›
Leagues ranked by composite score — a weighted sum of per-90 stats for all eligible players (min. ${MIN} mins played): Goals ×28 · Assists ×20 · Prog passes ×2.2 · Duels won ×1.8 · Tackles ×2.5 · Pressures ×1.2 · Interceptions ×2.0. All figures are per 90 minutes, weighted by minutes played.

Methodology
[5]Composite score: Goals (×28) + Assists (×20) + Prog passes (×2.2) + Duels won (×1.8) + Tackles (×2.5) + Pressures (×1.2) + Interceptions (×2.0). Weights provisional.
[6]Eligibility: > 60 mins played. Player counts reflect only those who have appeared on the pitch — squad members yet to play are not included. Counts will rise as the tournament progresses. Avg player / 90 = composite score ÷ average 90s played per eligible player. Excludes eliminated leagues.
[7]Progressive passes: moves ball ≥10 yds toward goal or into penalty area (excl. defensive third). Opta definition via FBref.
